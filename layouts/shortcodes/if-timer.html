5:2 Protocol: Eat normally 5 days a week. On 2 non-consecutive days, restrict calories to 500–600 kcal (women/men respectively). No specific eating window required on fast days — spread calories however works best for you. Many people find it easier to have one small meal rather than two.

Black coffee, plain tea, and water are generally allowed during the fasting window without breaking a fast. Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium) can help with keto + IF combined.
